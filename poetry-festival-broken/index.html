The Altamont Poetry Festival

A tradition, since 1975.

What is the Altamont Poetry Festival?

The Altamont Poetry Festival is a tradition that has been held in Altamont, New York since 1975. The festival is held every year in October, and is a great way to meet new people and get to know the community.
